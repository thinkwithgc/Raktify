About Raktify

Raktify is a free, non-profit blood-donor network that connects voluntary blood donors, hospitals and blood banks across India — so that no emergency blood request goes unanswered. It is operated by Choudhari EduHealth India Foundation, a registered NGO (NGO-DARPAN MH/2025/0643345, 80G eligible) based in Amravati, Maharashtra. Learn more: How Raktify works.
