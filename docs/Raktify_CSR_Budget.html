Funding a permanent, life-saving digital public good

Raktify is a free platform that connects voluntary blood donors, hospitals, and blood banks so no emergency request goes unanswered. The platform is built and launch-ready. This sheet sets out the relaxed (conservative-high) cost of running and sustaining it for two years — and the impact a CSR partnership unlocks.

Headline ask
Where the platform is today, and what comes next

The platform is code-complete on the full 8-phase MVP — donor & institutional onboarding, blood-bank inventory with TTI screening, request & matching engine, coordinator escalation rings, hemovigilance reporting, audit-hash chain, donor badges, camp scheduling, thalassemia & rare-blood registries. The following items are scoped, designed, and partly stubbed; CSR funding accelerates each from "ready to wire" to "in production".
