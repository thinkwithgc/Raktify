Two-Year Operating Budget & 18-Month Roadmap Prepared for CSR partnership
21 May 2026  ·  FY2026–FY2028

Funding a permanent, life-saving digital public good

Raktify is a free, India-first platform connecting voluntary blood donors, hospitals, blood banks and camp organizers so no emergency request goes unanswered. The full 8-phase MVP is code-complete and live on Azure at raktify.choudhari.ngo. This sheet sets out the two-year operating cost and the 18-month aggressive roadmap that a CSR partnership unlocks — from district roll-out to pan-India coverage, Aadhaar KYC, IoT cold-chain, insurance integration, and federated rare-blood broadcasting.

Headline ask
Where the platform is today

The full 8-phase MVP is code-complete and live on Azure. 44 database migrations, 44 API endpoints, five role portals (donor, hospital, blood bank, coordinator, NGO admin), 10-tab admin console, end-to-end camp lifecycle with public host applications + NGO verification + magic-link organizer dashboards + multi-channel share + per-channel attribution, thalassemia + rare-blood registries, hash-chained audit log, DHO hemovigilance report export. RLS-gated, column-encrypted, demo-ready at raktify.choudhari.ngo.

The roadmap below is what CSR funding turns from "designed and stubbed" into "live and serving patients". Every line item is finite and scoped — no greenfield research, no speculative bets.

cost to donors, hospitals, blood banks, camp hosts — Raktify is free to use, always
  • The funding builds rails, not transactions — a permanent digital public good that keeps saving lives every year after launch, not a one-time camp.
  • Raktify is built to absorb existing offline networks — WhatsApp groups, paper rosters, ad-hoc Rotary drives. Every community onboarded multiplies reach at near-zero marginal cost.
  • A hash-chained audit trail, RLS-gated PII, AES-GCM column encryption and lab-verified-only matching make Raktify a safe, accountable system a CSR partner, a hospital chain, or a state health mission can stand behind.
  • The platform is white-label ready — the same codebase can power a partner NGO's deployment in a different state without code duplication.
  • CSR contribution is fully 80G-eligible and aligned with CSR Schedule VII (i) preventive healthcare & (xii) disaster management preparedness.

Figures are conservative-high two-year planning estimates. Cloud and communications costs scale linearly with adoption; the Year-1 estimate assumes the Q3-2026 target of 3 districts · 500 donors · 50 emergency requests / month. Year-2 doubles. The "cost per request" figure is illustrative, dependent on district roll-out and adoption targets. Awareness drives, branded merchandise and partner event costs are funded by the Choudhari Foundation's general programme budget, not from CSR contribution scope.
